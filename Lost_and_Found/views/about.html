About Us

The CU Boulder Lost & Found web application was designed for students, by students. Student Developers, Alex Sorenson, Andrew Settergren, Ian Wong, Kelsey Valencia, and Nikkii Fox are all currently Computer Science majors here at CU! We have partnered with CU's Office of Information Technology to create a new secure resource for students who have lost or found items on campus.

How It Works




Login to your account


To post or search for a listing, please create an account on our portal using your CU email and login to your account.


Create a listing


Create a listing for the lost or found item and submit all the details.


Let us do the work!


Your listing will go live and be viewable to thousands of your fellow Buff peers. Once a listing is found matching the details of your lost item listing, we'll notify you!
